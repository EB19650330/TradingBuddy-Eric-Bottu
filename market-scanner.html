Futures = perpetual contracts on Binance, OKX and Hyperliquid (Hyperliquid perps are USDC-margined — check "USDC" above to see them). MEXC, Bitvavo and Kraken don't offer futures here, so they stay Spot-only.
Scanning multiple timeframes multiplies the number of requests — matches show which timeframe triggered them. Not every exchange natively supports every interval; unsupported ones fall back to the nearest one they do.
Approximated from EMA20 vs EMA50 on each pair's own candles — an extra filter on top of your strategy conditions, not a replacement for them.
Plays a short beep when an auto-refresh scan finds a match that wasn't there before.
Pre-filled with a public proxy (corsproxy.io) so MEXC works out of the box — but a third party sees those requests, so it's a stopgap, not a permanent fix. For your own setup, deploy a small proxy you control (a one-route Cloudflare Worker/Vercel function forwarding the request with Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *) and paste its URL here instead.
